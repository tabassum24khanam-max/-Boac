Our Story

A boarding gate for coffee lovers

BOAC Café takes its name and spirit from the golden age of travel — a place where every cup feels like a small departure. Tucked on Khurais Road in Al Rawdah, we pour specialty coffee for early risers, night owls and everyone in between.

Our baristas brew with intent, our kitchen bakes fresh through the day, and our room is built for lingering — whether you're catching up with friends or catching up on work. No rush, no last call.
