Our Story

A quiet room,
built around a cup.

"بواك" — because the right cup can change an entire morning.

Tucked along Khurais Road in Al Rawdah, BOAC is part coffee bar, part calm — cream plaster arches, warm wood, rattan light and the smell of fresh espresso. We roast for clarity, bake fresh through the day, and never close the door.
